Graffiti Freedom

2019 · VR Game

Graffiti Freedom is a VR experience that allows players to spray graffiti freely inside a virtual subway station—reclaiming public space without violating real-world laws.

By blending street art with immersive technology, the work explores freedom of expression, civic resistance, and the tension between legality and creativity in urban environments.
