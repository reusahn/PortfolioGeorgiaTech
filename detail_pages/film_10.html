The Way To Be Oneself

2025 · AI Film · 15min

This work is a 15 minute AI experimental film that follows the transition of a self observing protagonist trying to return to his original self. Troubled by unresolved thoughts he begins a thought experiment as he falls asleep, only to wake and find his body mysteriously returned to its proper orientation. Believing himself unwell he seeks medical consultation where conversations with a doctor fracture him into past present and future selves turning him from himself into an observer. As the boundary between observer and observed dissolves and even his name fades he rediscovers a friend then friendship then love. These sensations rather than diagnoses or explanations guide him back to himself. In the final scene he forgets that happiness again and restarts the experiment suggesting that the way of being oneself is endlessly reintroduced.
