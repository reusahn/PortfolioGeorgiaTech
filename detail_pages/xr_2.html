NARCI

2023 · VR Experience · The Cave Seoul

NARCI is an immersive VR experience that places the viewer inside an elevator, where they come face-to-face with various narcissistic figures.

Through disorienting encounters and gaslighting-like dialogue, the viewer’s self-esteem begins to erode—mirroring the psychological manipulation of toxic relationships.

However, the journey leads to a moment of confrontation with oneself, inviting healing and self-restoration. NARCI explores the emotional damage caused by narcissists, while empowering participants to reclaim their sense of self.
