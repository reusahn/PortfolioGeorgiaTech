Escape Metro

2024 · VR Game · Seongsu KAIA Pop-up Store

Escape Metro is a surreal VR escape game set in an uncanny subway system.

Players must solve puzzles and engage in real-time conversations with an in-game AI—powered by ChatGPT—to navigate the disorienting space and uncover the hidden logic of their entrapment.

The game was showcased at the Seongsu KAIA pop-up store as part of a Web3-integrated experience, exploring themes of isolation, control, and human-AI dialogue within a metaphorical transit system.
